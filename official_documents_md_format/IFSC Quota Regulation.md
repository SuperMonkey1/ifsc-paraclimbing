1. 
IFSC Climbing World Cups quotas 
1.1.  Currently eligible National Federations may, within the published deadlines, register Team Officials at 
any World Cup event in the capacity and number set out in Table 1 (Team Officials)  
1.2. Currently eligible National Federations may, within the published deadlines, register for each gender 
category and discipline of the relevant World Cup event as follow:  
1.2.1. Up to two (2) currently-eligible competitors;   
1.2.2. In addition to the individuals covered above, up to an additional four (4) competitors where the 
National Federation is the host nation; and  
1.2.3. an additional number of competitors where the National Federation has an Additional Quota in 
a relevant gender category and discipline, up to the number of that Additional Quota.  
1.3. The Additional Quota for each National Federation in each gender category and discipline will be 
determined by the number of competitors from each National Federation ranked between 1st and 40th 
in the relevant World Ranking as at the end of the previous year using the values set out in Table 2 
(Additional Quota).  
1.4. The IFSC will publish annually the calculated Additional Quota in each gender category and discipline for 
each relevant National Federation not later than 31 January each year. Any subsequent 
additions/deletions from this list will require the approval of the IFSC Executive Board and will be 
published on the IFSC website and communicated directly to all National Federations by the IFSC Sport 
Department.

2. 
IFSC Climbing World Championships quotas 
2.1.  Currently eligible National Federations may, within the published deadlines, register Team Officials at 
the IFSC Climbing World Championships in the capacity and number set out in Table 3 (Team Officials)  
2.2.  Currently eligible National Federations may, within the published deadlines, register for each gender 
category and discipline of the IFSC Climbing World Championships as follow:  
2.2.1. Up to two (2) currently-eligible competitors;  
2.2.2. Any current World Champion, in the Discipline for which they are champion;  
2.2.3. an additional number of competitors where the National Federation has an Additional Quota in 
a relevant gender category and discipline, up to the number of that Additional Quota.  
2.3. 
The Additional Quota for each National Federation in each gender category and discipline will be 
determined by the number of competitors from each National Federation ranked between 1st and 40th 
in the relevant World Ranking as at the end of the previous year using the values set out in Table 4 
(Additional Quota).  
2.4. 
For the avoidance of doubts, the Additional quotas that will be granted at the IFSC Climbing World 
Championships will be always fixed to forty (40), respecting the maximum number of quotas per 
National Federation. If any National Federation will reach the maximum number of additional quotas 
(as set out in Table 4), the remaining quotas will be allocated to the following ranked athletes (i.e. 41st 
rank, 42nd rank, etc.).  
 
2.5  
The IFSC will publish annually the calculated Additional Quota in each gender category and discipline 
for each relevant National Federation not later than 31 January each year. Any subsequent 
additions/deletions from this list will require the approval of the IFSC Executive Board and will be 
published on the IFSC website and communicated directly to all National Federations by the IFSC Sport 
Department.
